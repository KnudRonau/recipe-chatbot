name: Spaghetti with Meatballs,
cooking_time: 45,
ingredients:
1 lb ground beef,
1/2 cup bread crumbs,
1/2 cup milk,
1 egg,
1/2 onion, finely chopped,
1 clove garlic, minced,
1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese,
1 tsp dried basil,
1 tsp dried oregano,
Salt and pepper, to taste,
1 lb spaghetti,
1 jar tomato sauce
guide: 1. In a large bowl, combine the ground beef, bread crumbs, milk, egg, onion, garlic, Parmesan cheese, basil, oregano, salt, and pepper. Mix well and form into meatballs.\n2. Heat a large skillet over medium heat and add the meatballs. Cook until browned on all sides, about 10 minutes.\n3. Add the tomato sauce to the skillet and simmer for 30 minutes.\n4. Cook the spaghetti according to package instructions. Serve the meatballs and sauce over the spaghetti.

name: Chicken Stir-Fry,
cooking_time: 30,
ingredients: 
1 lb boneless, skinless chicken breast, sliced,
2 tbsp soy sauce,
1 tbsp cornstarch,
1 tbsp vegetable oil,
1 onion, sliced,
2 bell peppers, sliced,
1 cup sliced mushrooms,
1 cup broccoli florets,
1 tbsp minced ginger,
2 cloves garlic, minced,
Salt and pepper, to taste,
Cooked rice, for serving
guide: 1. In a small bowl, whisk together the soy sauce and cornstarch. Set aside.\n2. Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet over high heat. Add the chicken and stir-fry for 3-4 minutes, until browned on all sides.\n3. Add the onion, bell peppers, mushrooms, broccoli, ginger, and garlic to the skillet. Stir-fry for another 3-4 minutes, until the vegetables are tender.\n4. Add the soy sauce mixture to the skillet and stir until the sauce thickens and coats the chicken and vegetables. Season with salt and pepper to taste.\n5. Serve the stir-fry over cooked rice.

name: Roasted Salmon,
cooking_time: 20,
ingredients: 
4 salmon fillets,
1 lemon, sliced,
4 sprigs fresh thyme,
2 tbsp olive oil,
Salt and pepper, to taste
guide: 1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).\n2. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.\n3. Arrange the salmon fillets on the baking sheet and season with salt and pepper.\n4. Drizzle the olive oil over the salmon and top each fillet with a slice of lemon and a sprig of thyme.\n5. Bake for 15-20 minutes, until the salmon is cooked through and flakes easily with a fork.\n6. Serve the roasted salmon with your favorite sides.

name: Beef Stew,
cooking_time: 120,
ingredients: 
2 lbs beef chuck, cut into 1-inch chunks,
1/4 cup all-purpose flour,
1 tsp salt,
1/2 tsp black pepper,
2 tbsp vegetable oil,
2 onions, chopped,
4 garlic cloves, minced,
2 cups beef broth,
1 cup red wine,
1 can diced tomatoes,
3 carrots, sliced,
3 potatoes, diced,
2 bay leaves,
2 tsp dried thyme
guide: 1. Preheat the oven to 325°F (165°C).\n2. In a large bowl, combine the flour, salt, and black pepper. Dredge the beef in the flour mixture.\n3. Heat the vegetable oil in a Dutch oven over medium-high heat. Add the beef and brown on all sides, about 10 minutes. Remove the beef from the pot and set aside.\n4. Add the onions and garlic to the pot and cook until softened, about 5 minutes.\n5. Add the beef broth, red wine, diced tomatoes (with their juices), carrots, potatoes, bay leaves, and thyme to the pot. Stir to combine.\n6. Return the beef to the pot and bring the mixture to a boil. Cover the pot with a lid and transfer to the oven.\n7. Bake for 1 1/2 to 2 hours, until the beef is tender and the vegetables are cooked through.\n8. Remove the bay leaves and serve the beef stew hot.
        
name: Mushroom Risotto,
cooking_time: 45,
ingredients: 
6 cups chicken broth,
1 lb mushrooms, sliced,
4 tbsp butter,
1 onion, chopped,
1 1/2 cups Arborio rice,
1/2 cup white wine,
1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese,
Salt and pepper, to taste
guide: 1. In a saucepan, bring the chicken broth to a simmer.\n2. In a large skillet, melt 2 tbsp of the butter over medium-high heat. Add the mushrooms and cook until browned, about 5-7 minutes. Remove the mushrooms from the skillet and set aside.\n3. In the same skillet, melt the remaining 2 tbsp of butter. Add the onion and cook until softened, about 5 minutes.\n4. Add the Arborio rice to the skillet and stir to coat with the butter and onions. Cook for 1-2 minutes, until the rice is toasted.\n5. Add the white wine to the skillet and stir until the wine has been absorbed by the rice.\n6. Add the chicken broth to the skillet, one ladleful at a time, stirring constantly and allowing each ladleful to be absorbed before adding the next.\n7. After about 20-25 minutes, the rice should be tender and creamy. Stir in the grated Parmesan cheese and reserved mushrooms. Season with salt and pepper to taste.\n8. Serve the mushroom risotto hot.
        
name: Beef and Broccoli,
cooking_time: 30,
ingredients: 
1 lb flank steak, sliced,
1/4 cup soy sauce,
1 tbsp cornstarch,
1 tbsp vegetable oil,
3 cups broccoli florets,
2 cloves garlic, minced,
1 tsp grated ginger,
1/2 cup beef broth,
1 tbsp brown sugar,
Cooked rice, for serving
guide: 1. In a bowl, whisk together the soy sauce and cornstarch. Add the sliced flank steak and toss to coat.\n2. Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add the sliced steak and cook for 2-3 minutes on each side, until browned. Remove the steak from the skillet and set aside.\n3. In the same skillet, add the broccoli florets, garlic, and ginger. Cook for 2-3 minutes, until the broccoli is bright green and tender.\n4. In a small bowl, whisk together the beef broth and brown sugar. Add the beef broth mixture to the skillet and stir to combine.\n5. Return the cooked steak to the skillet and stir to combine. Cook for an additional 1-2 minutes, until the sauce has thickened and the steak is cooked through.\n6. Serve the beef and broccoli hot over cooked rice.
        
name: Pesto Pasta,
cooking_time: 20,
ingredients: 
1 lb spaghetti,
1 cup fresh basil leaves,
1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese,
1/2 cup pine nuts,
2 garlic cloves, minced,
1/2 cup olive oil,
Salt and pepper, to taste
guide: 1. Cook the spaghetti according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.\n2. In a food processor, combine the basil, Parmesan cheese, pine nuts, and garlic. Pulse until the ingredients are finely chopped.\n3. With the food processor running, slowly drizzle in the olive oil until a smooth paste forms.\n4. Season the pesto with salt and pepper to taste.\n5. Toss the cooked spaghetti with the pesto until well coated.\n6. Serve the pesto pasta hot, topped with additional Parmesan cheese and pine nuts, if desired.
        
name: Teriyaki Chicken,
cooking_time: 30,
ingredients: 
1 lb chicken thighs, boneless and skinless,
1/2 cup soy sauce,
1/4 cup brown sugar,
2 tbsp honey,
2 tbsp rice vinegar,
1 tbsp sesame oil,
2 garlic cloves, minced,
1/2 tsp grated ginger,
1 tbsp cornstarch,
2 tbsp water,
Sesame seeds and chopped green onions, for serving
guide: 1. In a bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, brown sugar, honey, rice vinegar, sesame oil, garlic, and ginger.\n2. Add the chicken thighs to the bowl and toss to coat. Marinate for at least 10 minutes.\n3. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add the chicken thighs and cook for 5-6 minutes on each side, until browned and cooked through.\n4. In a small bowl, whisk together the cornstarch and water. Add the cornstarch mixture to the skillet and stir to combine.\n5. Bring the mixture to a boil and cook for 1-2 minutes, until the sauce has thickened.\n6. Serve the teriyaki chicken hot, topped with sesame seeds and chopped green onions.

name: Spaghetti Carbonara,
cooking_time: 20,
ingredients: 
1 lb spaghetti,
4 oz pancetta, diced,
4 garlic cloves, minced,
4 eggs,
1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese,
Salt and pepper, to taste
guide: 1. Cook the spaghetti according to package instructions, until it is al dente.\n2. While the spaghetti is cooking, heat a large skillet over medium heat. Add the diced pancetta and cook for 5-6 minutes, until it is crispy and browned.\n3. Add the minced garlic to the skillet with the pancetta and cook for 1-2 minutes, until fragrant.\n4. In a small bowl, whisk together the eggs and grated Parmesan cheese.\n5. Drain the cooked spaghetti and add it to the skillet with the pancetta and garlic. Toss to combine.\n6. Pour the egg and Parmesan mixture over the spaghetti and toss to combine. The heat from the spaghetti should cook the eggs and create a creamy sauce.\n7. Season the spaghetti carbonara with salt and pepper to taste.\n8. Serve the spaghetti carbonara hot, garnished with additional grated Parmesan cheese and chopped parsley, if desired.

name: Beef Stroganoff,
cooking_time: 30,
ingredients: 
1 lb beef sirloin, thinly sliced,
1 onion, sliced,
2 garlic cloves, minced,
8 oz sliced mushrooms,
2 tbsp butter,
2 tbsp flour,
1 cup beef broth,
1 cup sour cream,
Salt and pepper, to taste,
Cooked egg noodles, for serving
guide: 1. In a large skillet, melt the butter over medium-high heat. Add the thinly sliced beef sirloin to the skillet and cook for 3-4 minutes on each side, until it is browned.\n2. Remove the beef from the skillet and set it aside.\n3. Add the sliced onion, minced garlic, and sliced mushrooms to the skillet. Cook for 5-6 minutes, until the vegetables are tender.\n4. Add the flour to the skillet and stir to combine. Cook for 1-2 minutes, until the flour is lightly browned.\n5. Slowly pour the beef broth into the skillet, stirring constantly to prevent lumps. Cook for 2-3 minutes, until the sauce has thickened.\n6. Add the cooked beef back to the skillet and stir to combine. Season with salt and pepper to taste.\n7. Remove the skillet from the heat and stir in the sour cream.\n8. Serve the beef stroganoff hot over cooked egg noodles.

name: Mushroom Risotto,
cooking_time: 45,
ingredients: 
1 lb mixed mushrooms, sliced,
1 onion, finely chopped,
2 garlic cloves, minced,
1 1/2 cups Arborio rice,
1/2 cup dry white wine,
4 cups chicken broth,
1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese,
2 tbsp butter,
2 tbsp olive oil,
Salt and pepper, to taste
guide: 1. In a large saucepan, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the sliced mushrooms to the saucepan and cook for 5-6 minutes, until they are browned and tender.\n2. Remove the mushrooms from the saucepan and set them aside.\n3. In the same saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the finely chopped onion and minced garlic to the saucepan and cook for 2-3 minutes, until the onion is translucent.\n4. Add the Arborio rice to the saucepan and stir to combine. Cook for 1-2 minutes, until the rice is lightly toasted.\n5. Pour the dry white wine into the saucepan and stir to combine. Cook for 2-3 minutes, until the wine has been absorbed by the rice.\n6. Slowly pour the chicken broth into the saucepan, 1 cup at a time, stirring constantly to prevent lumps. Cook for 20-25 minutes, until the rice is tender and the liquid has been absorbed.\n7. Stir the cooked mushrooms and grated Parmesan cheese into the risotto. Season with salt and pepper to taste.\n8. Serve the mushroom risotto hot, garnished with chopped fresh parsley or thyme, if desired.

name: Stuffed Bell Peppers,
cooking_time: 60,
ingredients: 
4 bell peppers,
1 pound ground beef,
1 onion, chopped,
3 cloves garlic, minced,
1 can diced tomatoes,
1 cup cooked rice,
1 tablespoon olive oil,
1 teaspoon Italian seasoning,
Salt and pepper, to taste
guide: 1. Preheat oven to 375°F. \n2. Cut off the tops of the bell peppers and remove the seeds and membranes. \n3. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium-high heat. \n4. Add ground beef, onion, and garlic to skillet and cook until beef is browned. \n5. Drain any excess fat from skillet. \n6. Place stuffed bell peppers in a baking dish and bake for 30-40 minutes or until peppers are tender. \n9. Serve hot.
